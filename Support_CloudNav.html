About CloudNav

Welcome to the support page for CloudNav, a private, offline-first GPS utility designed for precision. CloudNav is designed to be private by default—no account required, no tracking, and all data stays on your device.

Key Features

  • Live GPS data including coordinates, altitude, speed, and heading
  • Supports both decimal and degrees-minutes-seconds (DMS) formats
  • Save locations locally on your device
  • View saved locations on an interactive map
  • Quickly copy or share coordinates and location details
  • Generate emergency-ready coordinate messages
  • Search and organize saved locations with a clean logbook
  • Works in low-connectivity and offline scenarios

Privacy First

CloudNav is designed to be private by default:

  • No account required
  • No tracking
  • All data stays on your device

FAQs

Check our frequently asked questions below for quick answers:

  • Can CloudNav work offline? Yes, CloudNav is designed to work in low-connectivity and offline scenarios. All data stays on your device.
  • What location data does CloudNav provide? CloudNav provides live GPS data including latitude, longitude, altitude, speed, heading, and accuracy in real time.
  • Does CloudNav require an account? No, CloudNav requires no account and is private by default. All your data stays on your device.
  • Can I save and view locations? Yes, you can save locations locally on your device and view them on an interactive map. You can also search and organize saved locations with the clean logbook feature.
  • What coordinate formats does CloudNav support? CloudNav supports both decimal and degrees-minutes-seconds (DMS) coordinate formats.
