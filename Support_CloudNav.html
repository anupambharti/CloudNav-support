Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for the most common CloudNav support questions.

Can CloudNav work offline?

Yes. CloudNav is designed for low-connectivity and offline scenarios. GPS availability depends on your device and environment, but your saved data remains on your device.

What location data does CloudNav provide?

CloudNav provides latitude, longitude, altitude, speed, heading, and accuracy readings in real time when your device has an active GPS fix.

Does CloudNav require an account?

No. CloudNav does not require an account, sign-in, or user profile.

Can I save and view locations?

Yes. You can save locations locally, search your logbook, and review saved places on an interactive map.

Which coordinate formats are supported?

CloudNav supports decimal degrees and degrees-minutes-seconds, commonly known as DMS.

Why does accuracy change?

GPS accuracy can change because of buildings, weather, terrain, indoor use, device hardware, and satellite visibility. A clearer sky view usually improves the fix.
